_证据：_ [LingBot-VA 2.0](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/07/11/ant-groups-robbyant-unveils-lingbot-va-2-0/)、[SensorFM](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/07/10/google-research-introduces-sensorfm-a-wearable-health-foundation-model-pretrained-on-one-trillion-minutes-of-sensor-data/)、[GPT-Live](https://www.marktechpost.com/2026/07/08/openai-releases-gpt-live-and-gpt-live-1-mini-full-duplex-voice-models-that-delegate-deeper-reasoning-to-gpt-5-5/)。
